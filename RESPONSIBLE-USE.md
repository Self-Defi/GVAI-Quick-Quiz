# GVAI Quick Quiz — Responsible Use Agreement

Use of this module signifies agreement to the following responsible-use guidelines. These principles protect students, educators, and the integrity of the GraduationVaultsAI ecosystem.

---

## 1. Respect Student Privacy
This module does not track or store data.  
Users agree not to implement modifications that:
- Log quiz answers
- Store user identities
- Transmit sensitive information

Privacy must remain intact at all times.

---

## 2. Maintain a Safe Learning Environment
Users agree to:
- Provide age-appropriate guidance  
- Ensure the module is used for constructive learning only  
- Encourage discussion and critical thinking about AI concepts  
- Prevent misuse, harassment, or inappropriate prompts  

---

## 3. No Misrepresentation
Users may not present this module as:
- Financial advice  
- An official assessment system  
- A certified curriculum  
- An investment or crypto training tool  

The module is strictly **educational**.

---

## 4. Keep the System Transparent
All modifications should maintain:
- Local-only execution  
- Zero-tracking architecture  
- Transparent documentation  

No hidden modules or non-disclosed data collection systems may be added.

---

## 5. Use AI Responsibly
Students should:
- Understand AI as a tool, not an authority  
- Recognize limitations and biases  
- Avoid using AI to plagiarize or replace genuine learning  
- Learn how to critically evaluate information

---

## 6. Allowed Uses
✔ Classroom demonstrations  
✔ Homework engagement  
✔ Microschool instructional units  
✔ Parent-guided study  
✔ Technical introduction modules  

---

## 7. Prohibited Uses
✘ Data collection  
✘ Commercial exploitation  
✘ Surveillance  
✘ Embedding into custodial or financial systems  
✘ Claims of certification or accreditation  

---

By using GVAI Quick Quiz, you support a responsible, transparent, and student-first introduction to AI literacy.
